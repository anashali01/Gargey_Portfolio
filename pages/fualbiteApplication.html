SUMMARY

Designed an end-to-end nutrition tracking app focused on simplifying complex food logging and body transformation progress. Built intuitive flows, high-fidelity UI, and a scalable design system to support AI-powered meal analysis and personalized nutrition guidance.

Problem Statement

People want to eat better and see progress.

But complex numbers, daily logging, and cluttered apps make it frustrating.

So most give up before results ever show.

solution

FuelBite was designed to make nutrition feel simple and doable.

Instead of complex tracking and manual inputs, it focuses on guidance, clarity, and small wins

With photo-based logging, clear goals, and visual progress, the experience helps users stay motivated and keep moving forwardâ€”one step at a time.
